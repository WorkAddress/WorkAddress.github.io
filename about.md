# About Me

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering at the Georgia Institute of Technology, with an expected graduation in May 2028. I was honored to be recognized as a Gold Scholar, a designation awarded to the top 2% of incoming students.

Growing up around academia, with my mother being a professor herself, instilled in me a deep appreciation for continuous learning and rigorous research. This foundation drove my early interest in complex problem-solving, leading to a strong focus on Java, data structures, and the intersection of physics and computing. In the fall of 2025, I took a major step toward formalizing my research experience by interviewing for a spot in Professor Li's lab to further apply these concepts.

When I am not working on coursework, engineering electromechanical systems, or debugging computer vision pipelines, you can usually find me grinding stats on my Old School RuneScape ironman account or catching up on JoJo's Bizarre Adventure.
