# About Me

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering at the Georgia Institute of Technology, with an expected graduation in May 2028. I was honored to be recognized as a Gold Scholar, a designation awarded to the top 2% of incoming students.

Growing up around academia, with my mother being a professor herself, instilled in me a deep appreciation for continuous learning and rigorous research. This foundation drove my early interest in complex problem-solving, leading to a strong focus on Java, data structures, and the intersection of physics and computing.

When I am not working on coursework, engineering electromechanical systems, or debugging computer vision pipelines, you can usually find me grinding stats on my [Old School RuneScape ironman account](osrs.html) or on practicing my Video Editing skills.
